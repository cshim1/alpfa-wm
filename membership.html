The Value

Why Join ALPFA W&M?

We are a launchpad, not just a club. Everything we offer is designed to move you closer to where you want to be.

Professional Network

Build relationships with 100K+ ALPFA members nationwide, including professionals at Fortune 500 companies. Your network is your net worth.

Career Development

Access resume workshops, mock interviews, LinkedIn clinics, and mentorship from professionals who have walked the path you're on.

Community & Culture

Find your people. A space where your background is a strength, not something to minimize. Latino-based, but open to all who share our values.

Internship Pipeline

Get connected to internship and full-time opportunities through our sponsor network. KPMG, PwC, EY, and Becker actively recruit through our chapter.

National Conferences

Attend ALPFA's national conference and regional events. Represent W&M on a national stage alongside thousands of Latino professionals and students.

Leadership Experience

Take on board roles, lead initiatives, and build the leadership credentials that set you apart in recruiting. Employers notice chapter leadership.

Get Started

How to
Join Us

Membership is open to all William & Mary students regardless of major, year, or background. All you need is the drive to grow professionally and a commitment to showing up.

To join, reach out to us directly or attend one of our general body meetings. We also recruit during organization fairs at the start of each semester.

There is a one-time national ALPFA membership fee that gives you access to the full national platform, job board, and conference registration discounts.
