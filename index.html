Our Purpose

A Launchpad
for Leaders

ALPFA at William & Mary is more than a student organization. We are a community where ambition becomes achievement — empowering Latino students and allies to develop the skills, mindset, and network needed to thrive.

Every event, every connection, every workshop is designed to move you forward.
