Our Story

Born at
William & Mary

Founded in 2023, ALPFA at William & Mary emerged from a simple belief: Latino students deserve a dedicated space to grow professionally, build community, and connect with the opportunities they have earned.

What started as a vision shared between a handful of ambitious students has become the premier professional development organization for Latino students and allies at W&M.

In just two years, our chapter has grown to 47 members, hosted dozens of events, and built lasting partnerships with industry leaders like KPMG, PwC, EY, and Becker.

What Drives Us

Mission &
Values

ALPFA at William & Mary is more than a student organization — we are a launchpad for leadership, confidence, and career readiness. We empower Latino students and allies to develop the skills, mindset, and network needed to thrive across industries.

We operate on four core values: excellence, community, integrity, and impact. These principles guide everything from how we run meetings to how we represent the ALPFA brand at national conferences.

The Bigger Picture

ALPFA
National

The Association of Latino Professionals For America is the largest Latino professional membership organization in the United States. With over 100,000 members and 200+ chapters across the country, ALPFA creates pathways for Latino professionals at every stage of their career.

As a W&M chapter member, you gain access to the full ALPFA national network — job boards, national conferences, mentorship programs, and career fairs with Fortune 500 companies.

Regional Connection

ALPFA
Washington DC

Our chapter maintains a close relationship with the ALPFA DC professional chapter, one of the most active and influential chapters in the country. Through this connection, W&M members gain access to networking events, professional mentors, and internship pipelines in the DMV area.

The DC chapter's proximity to the nation's capital means unparalleled access to government, consulting, finance, and policy opportunities — all within reach for our members.
